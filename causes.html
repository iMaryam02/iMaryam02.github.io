Causes Of Mental Health


There is no single cause for mental illness. A number of factors can contribute to risk for mental illness.

Mental disorders are not caused by character flaws. They have nothing to do with being lazy or weak. Changes in brain chemistry from substance abuse or changes in diet can also cause mental disorders
