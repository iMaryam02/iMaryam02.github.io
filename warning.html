Warning Signs Of Mental Health


Signs and symptoms of mental illness can vary, depending on the disorder, circumstances and other factors.

Mental illness symptoms can affect emotions, thoughts and behaviors. Sometimes symptoms of a mental health disorder appear as physical problems, such as stomach pain, back pain, headaches, or other unexplained aches and pains.

Signs Of Mental Health


  • Feeling sad or down

  • Confused thinking or reduced ability to concentrate

  • Excessive fears or worries, or extreme feelings of guilt

  • Withdrawal from friends and activities

  • Significant tiredness, low energy or problems sleeping

  • Detachment from reality delusions, paranoia or hallucinations

  • Inability to cope with daily problems or stress

  • Problems with alcohol or drug use

  • Excessive anger, hostility or violence

  • Suicidal thinking
